About Me

Hello! I'm Sasha Persaud, a passionate software engineer with a strong foundation in both software development and geographic information systems (GIS). I recently graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering and a minor in GIS. With a diverse set of technical skills ranging from Python and JavaScript to AWS and ArcGIS, I am dedicated to creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology and real-world challenges.

Throughout my academic journey and professional experiences, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of impactful projects. From building a Modbus server for digital electricity transmitters at Voltserver Inc. to leading a team of interns at TRC Companies, I thrive in collaborative environments where I can both contribute and learn.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am driven by a commitment to continuous learning and adaptability. Whether it's improving data processing scripts, developing embedded systems, or conducting GIS research in Rwanda, I am always eager to take on new challenges and grow as a professional.

I am open to new opportunities and willing to relocate for the right role. If you're looking for a software engineer who is not only technically proficient but also passionate about making a difference, I'd love to connect!
